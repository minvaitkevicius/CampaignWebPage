Before we start we will have a session 0 - to finish creating our characters, lay some custom rules out and to decide on some rules.

## Custom Rules/Tips

- IN THE JUNGLE - resting is difficult as you need a comfortable camp, LONG RESTS take 7 DAYS of gametime in the wilds, or 1 night if in a settlement, safe cave etc.

- For balancing this means that SHORT RESTS in the jungle are only when you sleep at night.

- Due to this being a hex map exploration (you will explore the jungle while looking for clues hex by hex) - you might end up in areas which are too harsh on your characters - RUNNING and coming back later is always an option.

## Rules to decide:

- Water and Food tracking - the campaign is built to make collecting water and food as an important task every day. Do you want this added pressure, or do we just ignore this?

- **Character Death as Written the Jungle and further sections are very dangerous**, how do you want to treat this?:
    
    - **Meat Grinder**: let characters die easily and be prepared to have backups
    
    - **Character deaths are rare**: characters only die if they knowingly due something very risky or dangerous.
    
    - **No character death**: due to the age curse we can play that characters that die rise as undead - with some changes but same stats/class so you never have to change your character.
    
- Help from the guides - you will be able to hire guides in the port, how much do you want them to influence your decisions
    
    - **Guides stay out of our exploration** and only shares useful tips or information on where we are going. So we can explore and uncover the map ourselves.
     
    - **Guides actively help us** guide to specific destinations we ask for.
