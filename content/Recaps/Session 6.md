The party tries to question Xandala, but Artus is uninterested and flies away on his animated frozen pterodactyl. Xandala says that he is a dangerous man with a dangerous artifact on him and should be captured. They leave her tied up and investigate the ruins near the Camp.

The ruins feature a motif of a crocodile being carried by a man, after hearing the old story from their guide Žvirblis and Sara go inside to explore the ruined temple. In there they discover some traps that they try to avoid unsuccessfully. Finally, Tuna figures out that they should imitate the sculptures of the crocodile and carry each other on their backs. They easily pass the remaining traps and grab a very interesting Jug from the final room. As one of them jumps off the back of their friend - the temple begins to rumble, rocks start to fall and the party quickly escapes outside. They discover that the Jug is an Alchemy Jug that produces a special kind of liquid for each character that attunes to them.
![[alchjug.png]]
They take Xandala onto the canoe and head towards Camp Vengance. During their trip Xandala misty steps out of the boat and while tied up tries to run - she is knocked unconscious by the party and left there on the shore. 

Finally, they arrive at camp Vengeance and Kornelijus flashes his holy symbol to get trough the gate. Inside they see the camp in a poor state - water seeps in from the jungle and everything is covered in wet mud. They head to the main tent where they meet Commander Neil Breakbone - who is visibly stressed and tired. He orders them to take as many sick men of theirs as they can to the canoe and get them to Port Nyanzaru for treatment at once. Crystal convinces him to let them stay the night and head out in the morning, the party takes a long rest in an uncomfortable, wet, stinking yet relatively safe camp.
