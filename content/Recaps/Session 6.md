The party tried to question Xandala, but Artus showed little interest and flew away on his animated, frost-covered pterodactyl. Xandala claimed that he was a dangerous man carrying a dangerous artifact, and insisted he should be captured. They left her tied up and turned their attention to the nearby ruins.

The ruins displayed a recurring motif of a crocodile being carried by a man. After hearing the old story from their guide, Žvirblis and Sara ventured inside to explore the ruined temple. Inside, they encountered several traps, which they attempted to avoid—without much success. Eventually, Tuna deduced that they should mimic the sculptures of the crocodile being carried and carry each other on their backs. Following this method, they easily bypassed the remaining traps and reached the final chamber, where they found an intriguing jug. As one of them jumped down from their companion’s back, the temple began to rumble. Stones fell from above, and the party dashed outside just in time. They soon discovered the jug was an Alchemy Jug that produced a unique liquid for each character who attuned to it.
![[alchjug.png]]
With their prize in hand, they brought Xandala aboard their canoe and headed toward Camp Vengeance. During the journey, Xandala misty stepped out of the canoe, still bound, and tried to flee. The party quickly knocked her unconscious and left her on the shore.

Finally, they reached Camp Vengeance. Kornelijus flashed his holy symbol to gain entry through the gates. Inside, they found the camp in disrepair—water seeping in from the jungle, the ground churned into thick mud. They proceeded to the main tent, where they met Commander Niles Breakbone, who was clearly exhausted and under great strain. He ordered them to take as many of his sick men as possible to Port Nyanzaru for treatment immediately. Crystal persuaded him to allow them to rest overnight before departing. The party spent the night in the camp—wet, muddy, foul-smelling, but at least relatively safe.
