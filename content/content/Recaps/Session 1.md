The party all took on the expedition after quick introductions with Renara Veloth and got the map of the island. During a shopping spree they got in a scuffle with an old lady selling potions, when Tuna grabbed her hand - she disappeared into thin air. After this encounter Žvirblis had a really bad night and woke up feeling weaker than yesterday.


![[Pasted image 20250125221644.png]]


In the morning the party decided to not stay in Port Nyanzaru for long and set sail to Fort Beluarian. Choosing to avoid the pirates they paid up the tribute to Aremag - a giant dragon turtle to safely pass the bay.


![[Pasted image 20250125220647.png]]


At fort Beluarian Ezra tried to create a distraction for a stranger named Mikhail in exchange for guide fees and a charter of exploration from the order of flaming fist. However, Kornelijus and Tuna stopped Mikhail and he was imprisoned by Liara Portyr.


![[Pasted image 20250125220749.png]]


Nonetheless, adventurers were able to strike a deal with Liara - a charter for exploration in exchange for finding out who is leaving these new strange footprints south of the Fort Beluarian. 

The party found a guide - a druid from the emerald enclave named Qawasha with his vegepygmy companion named Žolius. Together with the guide they started their travel south - in search of the strange tracks. And to exterminate as much undead as possible.


![[Pasted image 20250125221009.png]]


During their 3 day trip they already saw enormous dinosaurs, and a too large horde of undead for them to take on. On the third day they came upon 3 dead halflings that were attacked by "terrorfolk" as they made camp in the already prepared camp, multiple zombies came up on them during the night.
