
This experimental variant of the alchemy jug resonates with those who attune to it, adapting its concoctions to their unique magic and personalities. It retains the base functions of a normal Alchemy Jug, but with enhanced capabilities tied to its attuned users.

### Base Functions 
Once per day, choose a liquid to generate from the following list:
- Acid (8 oz)
- Basic poison (0.5 oz)
- Beer (4 gallons)
- Honey (1 gallon)
- Mayonnaise (2 gallons)
- Oil (1 quart)
- Vinegar (2 gallons)
- Fresh water (8 gallons)
- Salt water (12 gallons)
- Wine (1 gallon)

###  Adaptive Infusions – Attuned User Benefits:

####  Žvirblis
- **Feature – Experimental Compound**: Once per day, the jug produces a healing potion (requires vial).
- **Tinker’s Delight**: When using a jug-produced substances in an alchemist’s tool check or crafting, gain **advantage**.
The stopper has rotating dials and a tiny pressure gauge. Smells like pure ethanol.
#### Crystal
- **Feature – Mood Wine**: Once per day, the jug produces 1 serving  sparkling, color-shifting wine.
  - Grants **1d6 temporary HP** to up to 6 creatures.
  - Grants **advantage on Charisma (Performance or Persuasion)** for 1 hour.
The stopper is now shaped like a theatrical comedy mask, and the spout occasionally hums with a chord when opened. It gives off a faint floral perfume.
####  Ezra
- **Feature – Infernal Resin**: Once per day, the jug produces 1 vial of hellish ichor.
  - Can coat a weapon to deal **+1 fire damage** for 1 minute.
  - Alternatively, can be poured onto an metal object to cast **Heat Metal** (1/day).
 The stopper resembles a stylized devil’s horn, and if held to the ear, it whispers a name no one can recognize. Smells of brimstone.
####  Sara
- **Feature – Swarm Nectar**: Once per day, the jug produces a jar of glowing nectar.
  - Calms insects or their  swarms for 1 hour.
  - Heals you or any insectoid creatures for **2d4 HP**.
  - Grants **advantage on Animal Handling** for 1 check and **advantage to Stealth** checks in the jungle for 1 hour.
The spout resembles a honeycomb pattern, and when opened, a few glowing beetles or spores briefly drift out before vanishing.
####  Kornelijus
- **Feature – Grandma’s Broth**: Once per day, the jug produces 4 servings of rich soup.
  - Each portion heals **1d6 HP** (bonus action).
  - One creature may remove **1 level of exhaustion** (once per day only).
Smells like bread.  A faded carving of two elderly figures holding hands appears on the bottom.
#### Tuna
- **Feature – Silver Lotus Tea**: Once per day, the jug produces 4 serving a calming herbal tea.
  - Grants **advantage on next Wisdom saving throw** (within 1 hour).
  - When used in a **Hands of Healing** feature, it also removes one **negative condition** (charmed, frightened, paralyzed — 1/day).
 The surface becomes smooth and black like obsidian, etched faintly with silver claw marks and falling lotus petals. The liquid smells of green tea.
